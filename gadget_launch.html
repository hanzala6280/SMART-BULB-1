SMART BULB : the new light of life

Introduction

A Smart Bulb is an advanced LED light bulb that can be controlled using a smartphone, voice assistant, or remote applications. It is more energy-efficient and offers features such as brightness control, color change, and scheduling. Smart bulbs make our homes modern, automated, and convenient.

Features of Smart Bulb

  • Remote Control using Wi-Fi or Bluetooth
  • Adjustable Brightness & Colors
  • Energy Efficient LED Technology
  • Works with Alexa, Google Assistant & Siri
  • Scheduling & Automation

Daily Uses

  1. Switch on lights automatically at night
  2. Change brightness for study or relaxation
  3. Set mood lighting with different colors
  4. Save energy with smart timers
  5. Control lights with voice commands
